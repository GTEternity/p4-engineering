Semester 2 Week 18

Funnel Supremacy

May 15th - 19th

So this week, we finished building our boat. By that I mean we decked out the bottom with tape. We also finished our paper roller coasters. We weren't able to get the 2nd loop in time, but it's fine because we added 3 ENTIRE FUNNELS. Let me explain: the funnel at the top is to give some freedom as to where we drop the marble. The funnel right after the track is to catch the marble no matter how far it flies or how much it curves in. The 3rd funnel is directly below the 2nd funnel and it's only function is to funnel the marble from a wide opening to a small pipe going down into the box. AND IT WORKED. Except we probably will get a few points off for not having the vertical loop but whatever, funnel supremacy.
