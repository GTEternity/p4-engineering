Semester 2 Week 16

I'd like a layer with oars

May 1st - 5th

This week, we got our tape, which means no more infinite tape hack (aka bringing your own from home). But, we manage to finish the 1st layer of our boat and we added the rectangular base for the 2nd. The carbord we got for it is quite strong but not quite long enough. So, we decided to make one of the triangles have an end sticking out the bottom to make up for the missing part of the rectangle. Also, we managed to make surprisingly good oars. But the highlight of the whole week was the Segregator. The Segregator is a labeled piece of cardboard that goes across the width of the ship at the middle to keep the shape of the ship and to reduce area that water can get in. We also used a single piece of cardboard as a base and put it diagonally, which made it surprisingly strong. Long live The Segregator (the boat one, not the racism one).
