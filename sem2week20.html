Semester 2 Week 20

We're kinda boated

May 30th - June 2nd

So this week, we finally tested our boats for the final. When we were taking our boat for testing, the top fin was missing, so we took off the other decorations and just had the boat go plain. So, we went to the CV pool and we were some of the first groups who were ready at the start. So, our group and three others went first, and Jonah and Towa were able to get in the boat without it sinking, which is a good start. They were the only ones who didn't sink almost immediately after the round starting, and they managed to get our boat all the way to one end without sinking. They then got out because the boat would probably sink on the way back and they didn't want to sink. Our boat was still in recognizable shape after. But now, I want to talk about the true MVP: The Segregator. Without the Segregator, the walls would've collapsed in on themselves, water would get in, and the boat would sink. The segregator saved our boat.
