Cancer Lake

January 18th

Today, we went into chemical engineering, and we had a challenge:

The problem: A lake has bromide, chlorine, and sunlight, which makes it carcinogenic.
The lake is 10 acres on its surface.
The lake has 60 million gallons of water.
The lake is used by 600k people

My solution: put $2,537 of microalgae in the lake. It will cover the entire lake in 7 days, thus cutting out the sunlight and not making it carcinogenic.
Real life solution: $136,000 of shade balls (400k).
