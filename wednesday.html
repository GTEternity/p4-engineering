Sketches and TinkerCAD

TinkerCAD is a website where you can use a 3D environment to design different things.
In our case, it's a Rugh Goldberg Machine
My design is:
A pulley holding 2 baseballs, and one of them goes onto the ramp.
The ramp has sideguards to stop the ball from falling off the sides.
The ball then rolls down onto a lever.
The lever then launches a pin up into a balloon.
The balloon will be held by a pole in the back.
