Week 6: Computer Engineering & Laptop Dissection

On Monday, we went into a new unit. I was ecstatic when I heard it was Computer Engineering. Computer Engineering is basically the programming and assembly of computer components to make a computer that does at least 1 task. In Computer Engineering, there are embedded systems, which are computers that are specially programmed and assembled to do 1 task. Think of video game controllers, or TV Remotes, and stuff like that.
