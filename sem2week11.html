Semester 2 Week 11

Egg Drop: Building and Testing

March 27th-31st 2023

This week, we started building our designs for the egg drop. Wednesday was entirely for building, and we managed to build the popsicle stick cage for the egg. We used a bunch of tape and string to to keep it all together. MVPs were absolutely Brandon, who was really good at tying through small spaces, and Towa, who was able to undo double-knots. Friday, we attached the straws, the parachute, and the balloon. It's not exactly accurate to the TinkerCAD, but it's pretty close.
