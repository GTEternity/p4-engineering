Week 2 Review

This week, we learned a lot about TinkerCAD and safety protocols when in the workshop. Starting off with TinkerCAD, we finished our designs having learned to be accurate with our measurements to save time in the future. We also made models of some home appliences, and I chose my red toaster oven. I then learned that making something detailed requires quite a bit amount of time, as I was only able to get a fraction of the detail that the real toaster had. I personally also learned that the challenges are only to be done in class, because when I saw the assignment for Challenge 2, I did it at home. However, when I did the challenge in class, I scrapped the old design, which helped make it better. The safety protocols including holding a majority of the tools with both hands and making sure to clamp everything down for a lot of tools. The one that was most focused on for those was the drill. One more thing about safety is that we have to yell "Masks down!" before welding, "Welding!" when welding, and "Done!" when done.
