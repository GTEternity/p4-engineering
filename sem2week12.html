Cardboat Boat Design

Apr. 3rd-7th 2023

So we had a new project for the next few weeks: making a cardboard boat. And not some flimsy tiny little thing that could barely hold a marble, I'm talking structurally sound actualy boat that holds two people. And we're going to race with them. I'm with Towa, Brandon, and Jonah again, and my original sketch was looking good until I realized how wack it was and I instead reverted to a diamond with 1.5ft walls. We decided on a different design in the TinkerCAD and we even made a net of it.
